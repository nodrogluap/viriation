Vaccine neutralization efficacy

  • The estimated vaccine effectiveness against R.1 strain SARS-CoV-2 infection among residents in a Kentucky skilled nursing facility was 66.2% (95% CI = 40.5%–80.8%) and among health care workers was 75.9% (95% CI = 32.5%–91.4%) in the cohort of only those 14d+ post-vaccination Pfizer booster dose. VE against symptomatic COVID-19 was 86.5% (95% CI = 65.6%–94.7%) among residents and 87.1% (95% CI = 46.4%–96.9%) among HCP. VE against hospitalization was 94.4% (95% CI = 73.9%–98.8%) among residents; no HCP were hospitalized. Three residents died, two of whom were unvaccinated (VE = 94.4%; 95% CI = 44.6%–99.4%).
