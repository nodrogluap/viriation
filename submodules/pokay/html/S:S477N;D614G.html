Reinfection

  • 11 cases of re-infection with the "Marseille-4" variant found within 1028 positive tests, where all of the earlier infections were from different strains.
    This lineage is also characterized by N:p.M234I;A376T (Fournier et al. (2021))

Symptom prevalence

  • Compared to the clade 20A strains that predominated during phase 1 between March and May 2020, the Marseille-4 variant (characterized by this mutation plus N:p.M234I;A376T) was associated with a lower frequency of cough, rhinitis, and olfactory and gustatory disorders.
    By contrast, hypoxemia was more frequent in patients infected with the Marseille-4 variant. (Fournier et al. (2021))

Trafficking
