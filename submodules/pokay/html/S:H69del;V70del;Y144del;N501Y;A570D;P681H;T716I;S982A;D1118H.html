  • Lineage B.1.1.7 spike–pseudotyped VSV was tested for neutralization vs Wuhan reference genome pseudotype in 40 sera collected 7 or 21 days post-booster dose.   • Vaccine elicited antibodies neutralized virus with the B.1.1.7 spike protein with titers similar to D614G virus. Serum specimens from individuals vaccinated with the BNT162b2 mRNA vaccine neutralized D614G virus with titers that were on average 7-fold greater than convalescent sera.
