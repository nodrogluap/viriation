SARS-CoV-2 Spike protein (S) gene S:p.K417T literature reference collection

ACE2 receptor binding affinity

  • Causes no detectable change in KD value for antibody binding of P22A-1D1.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • Causes no detectable change in KD value for antibody binding of P5A-1D2.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • Causes 57.1 fold decrease in KD value for antibody binding of P5A-3C8.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • Causes 2.9 fold decrease in KD value for antibody binding of P2C-1F11.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • Causes 1.1 fold decrease in KD value of antibody binding of ACE2.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • The K417T mutation decreased the affinity ~2 fold, mainly by decreasing the k(on) but also by increasing the k(off) as measured by surface plasmon resonance.
    (Barton et al. (2021))

Antibody epitope effects

  • No detectable fold drop in IC50 of P22A-1D1.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • No detectable fold drop in IC50 of P5A-1D2.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • No detectable fold drop in IC50 of P5A-3C8.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))
  • 2.6 fold increase in IC50 of P2C-1F11.
    (Zhang et al. (2021))

Gene expression increase

  • Experimentally, Spike gene expression increased 0.25 fold (Starr et al. (2020))

Monoclonal antibody serial passage escape

  • Escape mutation against monoclonal antibody LY-CoV016 (Starr et al. (2021))
  • In vitro selection against class 1 antibody C682 (Wang et al. (2021))

Pharmaceutical effectiveness

  • COR-101 lost ~16x binding against this isolated mutation.
    Estesevimab lost ~16x binding against this isolated mutation. m396 lost ~8x binding against this isolated mutation.
    (Engelhart et al. (2021))

Virion structure

  • Estimated free energy change (ddG) for this variant is -0.64 kcal/mol (i.e. destabilizing relative to wild type) (Spratt et al. (2021))
