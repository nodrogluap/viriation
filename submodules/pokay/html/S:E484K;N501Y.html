ACE2 receptor binding affinity

  • In the case of VOC B.1.1.7+E484K, the addition of the E484K mutation to N501Y further increased the affinity, to ~15 fold higher than WT RBD (KD ~5 nM), by further increasing the k(on) as measured by surface plasmon resonance. Because the higher k(on) could result in mass transfer limiting binding, we confirmed that the kinetic measurement for this variant was not substantially affected by varying levels of immobilization.

Vaccine efficacy

  • Protection against symptomatic infection caused by Beta/Gamma (assumed by combined presence of N501Y and E484K) was also higher with partial vaccination for mRNA-1273 (77%) than BNT162b2 (60%) and ChAdOx1 (48%), and full vaccination increased effectiveness for BNT162b2 (84%).
