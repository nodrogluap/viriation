Antibody epitope effects

  • Alters cleavage to occur between residues C15 and V16, thereby eliminating the C15-C136 disulfide bond – similarly to escape mutants at positions C15 and C136.
    This in turn decreases to varying degrees N terminal domain antigen recognition by supersite i mAbs S2L28, S2M28, S2X28, S2X333, 4A8. (McCallum et al. (2021))
