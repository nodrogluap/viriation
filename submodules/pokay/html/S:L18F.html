SARS-CoV-2 Spike protein (S) gene S:p.L18F literature reference collection

T cell evasion

  • This mutation causes reduced recognition by CD4 positive t-cells in people with HLA class 2.
    (Riou et al. (2021))

Antibody epitope effects

  • Massive reduction in S2L28 monoclonal antibody EC50 (i.e. ablated recognition), within antigenic supersite "i", but no effect on other mAbs within that supersite (McCallum et al. (2021))
  • Ablates neutralization by N-terminal-domain-targeting mAbs 4-19.
    Impairs neutralization by N-terminal-domain-targeting mAbs 4A8 and 2-17. (Wang et al. (2021))

Trafficking

  • Lentiviral pseudotyped with this individual mutation from B.1.351 was tested on ACE2.293T cells. Luciferase activity was measured two days postinfection, showing mild decrease in infection rate amongst the cells, suggetsing that this mutation does not contributing to cell entry fitness.
    (Tada et al. (2021))
